Nicholas Wong

Nicholas Wong is a UI/UX design student aspiring to be a UI/Product Designer who wants to connect with people via the designs he invisions and creates. He wants to be able to bring his designs into a personal approach which can correlate to people with different backgrounds. When Nicholas is not working on design you can find him admiring fashion, building model kits, practicing grafitti style, and gaming. Nicholas is currently studying UI/UX design under UC Berkley Extension working towards his certificate by creating projects such as mobile app design and web redesigns as a product designer.
